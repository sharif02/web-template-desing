Our Story

Osteria was born out of a simple desire: to recreate the loud, joyous, and delicious Sunday dinners of our childhood in Napoli. We opened our doors ten years ago with a tiny kitchen and a big heart.

Today, our Executive Chef Marco still arrives at 5 AM to make the pasta dough by hand. We believe that good food takes time, and great food relies on the freshest ingredients from local farms.

Chef Marco Rossi
